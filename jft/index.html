About me

I am an Ambivert person who likes to know new people, make friends, travel new places and do hangouts with friends and family, still needs her own space to feel her own existence, loves to think about random things or read a story book sitting at a quite place alone.

I have been brought up in Mymensingh, Dhaka, Bangladesh in a sweet family with parents and five loving sisters where I was the youngest and the most adored one. Currently I live in Dhaka with my beloved husband, caring parents-in-laws and a handsome brother-in-law.

I love to read and learn new things, let it be literature, psychology or science. Technology is my point of interest. Though I graduated in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), I still feel like I do have a long way to go to get out of my thirst about it and to feel like "I have gathered enough knowledge in this field". I dream to make a minimum contribution to the superbly fast-forwarding modern technology.
